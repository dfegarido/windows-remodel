Popular Residential Window Styles

Double-hung

Classic vertical sliding windows with two sashes that move independently for optimal ventilation control.

Casement

Hinged windows that open outward like a door, providing unobstructed views and excellent ventilation.

Bay and Bow

Angled three-panel windows that extend outward, creating additional interior space and panoramic views.

Picture

Ideal for capturing panoramic views, picture windows create bright, open spaces. These fixed windows maximize natural light and energy efficiency.

Sliding

Efficient and easy to operate, sliding windows work well in contemporary homes. Their horizontal operation makes them perfect for wide views and ventilation.

Specialty

We also install specialty shapes and custom windows to meet unique architectural needs or personal preferences.
